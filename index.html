History of Miner's Theater

The Miner’s Theatre is associated with the City’s first most important industry, mining. The first coal mine was sunk in Collinsville in 1857. The town grew with the industry and by 1886 a man could walk underground through connecting tunnels between the mines from one edge of the City limits to the other.
